Ever wonder why Perry constantly eating and sleeping? Perry is essentially killing time, hoping one day to reunite with its extinguishing platypus family.

Habitat:

Perry lives in the lush wetlands of Tasmania, Australia, where dense vegetation provides shelter and protection. Perry loves freshwater pond, he floats on calm waters and forages for food.

Appearance:

Platypus-look-alike, perfect circle eyes and perfect oval body. Perry has four tiny adorable legs and one wiggling tail with a slightly denser color than its body.

Key Characteristics:

  • Very cautious, so Perry only actively seek for food if it feels safe, or else it will float around to scout the surroundings.
  • At night, Perry immediately rush back shelter, scared, because the dark once took away its family.
  • When scouting for food, its legs and tails constantly wiggling so it can react fast if danger comes.
  • Even at night, in shelter, Perry still keeps its eyes open for caution.
  • Perry constantly practicing camourflag by changing its skin colors, preparing for danger.

Recent studies highlight Perry's complex behaviors, including cautious foraging, and incredibly precise hunting tactics, as it can lerp fastly toward a targeted food.
